PARTY NOW RENTALS — HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS
==========================================

The website works right now with styled placeholders. To show your real
photos, save each image into THIS "images" folder using the EXACT names
below (all lowercase). Refresh the page and they appear automatically.

LOGO
----
logo.png   -> Your "PARTY Now RENTALS" logo (transparent PNG is best).
              Replaces the text logo in the header and footer on its own.

HERO (big banner at the top)
----------------------------
hero.jpg   -> A wide, pretty event photo. Good pick: the elegant candlelit
              dinner OR the burgundy themed long table.

ABOUT (Melanie & Cathy box)
---------------------------
family.jpg -> Your family photo (the 2 sisters + kids in the matching
              "Party Now Rentals" shirts).

GALLERY (the "Recent Events" grid — 9 photos)
---------------------------------------------
g1.jpg -> Wedding ceremony, rows of white folding chairs (Spanish/white venue)
g2.jpg -> Baby shower long table, tan gauze runner, "Love in Bloom" arch
g3.jpg -> Elegant candlelit dinner, white calla lilies (nighttime)
g4.jpg -> Poolside white rectangle tables & chairs (daytime, palms)
g5.jpg -> Backyard reception, black round tables + tan umbrellas + white chairs
g6.jpg -> Cocktail hour, black high-top tables + lounge area
g7.jpg -> Elegant place setting, gold flatware + scalloped plates (pool view)
g8.jpg -> Kids birthday, round tables, tropical centerpiece + bounce house
g9.jpg -> Burgundy/red themed long table, candles, heaters & slide

TIPS
----
- Keep the names EXACTLY as above. JPG or PNG both fine (keep the .jpg name).
- Landscape photos look best for the hero and gallery.
- That's it — no code needed. Just drop the files in and refresh.
