Meet The Sisters

Meet Melanie & Cathy

We're Melanie and Cathy! Sisters, stay-at-home moms, and the hearts behind Party Now Rentals. We run our family owned event rental business with our little ones cheering us on every step of the way.

What started as a love for decorating birthdays and family gatherings quickly grew into a passion for helping others celebrate life's moments. Between mom life and family life, we pour our hearts into providing high quality, stylish event rentals for every occasion — from birthdays, baby showers, and graduations to weddings, corporate events, and everything in between!

At Party Now Rentals, we truly believe every celebration deserves to feel special, no matter the size. We handle the details so you can relax, soak it all in, and focus on what matters most — making sweet memories with the people you love.

Thank you so much for supporting a local, family owned business. We are so grateful. We can't wait to celebrate with you and help bring your vision to life ✨

Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Santa Clarita and proudly serve events throughout Southern California. Not sure if we reach you? Just call or text (626) 257-6514.

How do delivery and setup work?

We offer delivery, setup, and pickup for every order. Delivery and setup fees depend on your location, order size, and what you need — we'll spell it out in your quote, no surprises.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend reserving 2–4 weeks ahead, especially for weekends. Need it sooner? We can often do same-week — and sometimes even same-day — depending on availability. Just call or text (626) 257-6514.

What can I rent?

Tables (round, banquet & cocktail), white plastic and resin chairs, linens and table runners, outdoor heaters, full bar setups, and custom themed decor — plus more on request.

What types of events do you do?

Weddings, birthdays, baby showers, graduations, corporate events, and fully custom themed celebrations of any size.

How do I reserve my date?

Pick your date on our booking calendar right here on the site, or call/text us at (626) 257-6514. We'll confirm availability and send a quote.
